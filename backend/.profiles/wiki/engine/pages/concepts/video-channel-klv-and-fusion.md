# Video Channel, KLV, and Fusion

- `XVideoIO` answers how video assets are opened.
- `NXVideoView` answers how a chosen channel is rendered.
- `NXMpegTSAnalysis` answers how KLV metadata is extracted from MPEG2 TS payloads.
- `NXImageLayerVideo` answers when video is embedded into an image-driven display flow.

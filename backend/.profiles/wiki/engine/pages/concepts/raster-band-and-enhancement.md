# Raster Band and Enhancement Model

- `XDMBand` is the unit of raster-band processing.
- Grayscale versus RGB display is usually a composite-assembly question, not a view question.
- Histogram matching, sharpening, and noise interpolation are advanced raster operations and should be explained separately from simple load/display flows.
